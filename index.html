Change your Reality

IRIS: Wisdom and Virtue is an upcoming hardcore third-person action roguelike game combined with puzzles and platforming genres.

The game is being developed with Unreal Engine 5 to achieve modern AAA-style western graphics and a smoother gameplay experience.

Third-Person Combat

Players have two types of attack: light attack and power attack. Attack pattern will be different when equipping different types of weapons.

Players can also parry to stunt enemies and block attacks to prevent losing health.

Parkour

Players can climb, vault, and mantle different types of walls.

Grappling Hook

Most levels will have unique puzzle mechanisms with different combinations of numbers or patterns which are randomized in each run. (e.g. push/stack boxes, solve symbolic codes, etc.)

Bandits

Random enemies appear on every level to prevent players from making it through the run.

Each bandit will have their own appearance and weapon (melee/ranged) variations.

Bosses

Each boss will have unique attack patterns, including boss lores and motivations on their appearance.

Fragment of Memories

Each memory can give a permanent boost throughout the journey. They can be obtained after defeating and sparing any six distinct bosses.

Shop

Players can buy one-round or lifetime power boosts to support their journey. The main shop is available at the start and small shops are available at some points during the game. Prices are based on kills.

Shops may also increase player’s health, damage, skills, etc.

Exploration

After completing any realm, the player can choose one of 1-3 paths to go through any realm variants.
Some realms may have a shortcut to reach The Gateway.
Some realms are locked and can be accessed by buying temporary items in previous realms.

Puzzles

Most levels will have unique puzzle mechanisms with different combinations of numbers or patterns which are randomized in each run. (e.g. push/stack boxes, solve symbolic codes, etc.)

Hong Kong ranked 88th in the World Happiness Report 2025

The record highlights the struggles of many residents who feel sad, depressed, or overwhelmed, sometimes leading to tragic outcomes like suicide. Our game aims to shed light on these daily struggles, showing players that depression affects many while encouraging them to accept reality and move forward.

Inspired by the evolution of gaming, we are creating an AAA-style video game with highly detailed graphics and smooth gameplay to raise awareness of these issues. The game will immerse players in a realistic and emotional experience, blending impactful storytelling with cutting-edge technology.

The story follows a hopeless young adult with an unclear future who begins using IRIS (Integration of Reality Interchange System), a device that projects consciousness into an alternate reality. What begins as an escape soon becomes an intense confrontation with his darkest fears and past traumas, forcing him to face the universal challenges of life.

The game will feature procedural generation, dynamic environments, advanced AI behavior, and cinematic effects using Unreal Engine 5. Tools such as Blender, Metahuman, Houdini, Cascadeur, Mixamo, and Metatailor will enhance the development process. With a focus on realism and emotional depth, the game aspires to inspire empathy and resilience.
